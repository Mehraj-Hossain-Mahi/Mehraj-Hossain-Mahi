## 👨‍💻 About Me

Hi, I'm **Mehraj Hossain Mahi**, a Computer Science student and junior researcher from Bangladesh.  
My work focuses on **Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Mobile Application Development, and research-based software systems**.

I enjoy building practical AI solutions for real-world problems, especially in **healthcare, language processing, image classification, dataset development, and secure AI-based verification systems**.

- 🔬 Currently exploring **AI-based Healthcare Systems, Computer Vision, NLP, and Post-Quantum Cryptography**
- 📊 Interested in **Dataset Development, Model Evaluation, Research Writing, and Applied Machine Learning**
- 📱 Also working with **Flutter, Firebase, Python, Java, SQL, and LaTeX**
- 🎯 Goal: To develop impactful AI tools and publish research that solves real-world problems
